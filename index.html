Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

Yes, while we do accept walk-ins based on availability, we highly recommend booking your appointment at least 48 hours in advance to secure your preferred time and stylist.

What brands do you use for hair color?

We exclusively use premium, salon-grade brands such as L'Oréal Professionnel, Wella, and Olaplex to ensure the best results without damaging your hair.

Do you offer trial sessions for bridal makeup?

Absolutely! We offer paid trial sessions for brides-to-be so we can finalize the perfect look before your big day. The trial fee is adjusted if you book the final package with us.
