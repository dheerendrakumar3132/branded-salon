Our Treatments

Explore our comprehensive range of bespoke beauty, hair, and wellness therapies tailored exclusively for you.

Hair Artistry & Care

Your hair is your best accessory. Our master stylists use advanced techniques and world-class products like L'Oréal and Olaplex to create looks that complement your face shape and lifestyle.

  • Precision Cutting & Styling

    From classic bobs to trendy layers, we craft styles that are easy to maintain and visually stunning.

  • Advanced Coloring & Balayage

    Bespoke color formulas tailored to your skin tone, featuring damage-free highlighting and root-touchups.

  • Keratin & Botox Treatments

    Frizz-taming, intensive smoothing therapies that restore natural shine and repair damaged cuticles.

Bridal Studio & Makeup

Your big day deserves perfection. Our certified makeup artists specialize in creating flawless, long-lasting looks that look spectacular in person and on camera.

  • High-Definition (HD) Makeup

    Using premium brands like MAC, Huda Beauty, and Bobbi Brown, we create a natural, unblemished finish that lasts all day.

  • Airbrush Artistry

    A lightweight, silicone-based makeup sprayed softly onto the skin for a sweat-proof, tear-proof, and completely flawless canvas.

  • Pre-Bridal Packages

    Months of preparation condensed into relaxing sessions including full-body polishing, skin brightening, and hair nourishment.

Advanced Skincare

Radiant skin starts with healthy habits and professional care. We offer dermatologically tested treatments targeting everything from acne to anti-aging.

  • HydraGlow Deep Cleanse

    A multi-step treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, and extracts impurities while infusing skin with hydrating serums.

  • Anti-Aging & Lifting Therapies

    Collagen-boosting facials designed to tighten the skin, reduce fine lines, and restore a youthful plumpness.

  • De-Tan & Pigmentation Care

    Gentle yet effective treatments using Vitamin C and natural fruit extracts to even out skin tone and remove sun damage.

Luxury Spa & Wellness

Step away from the hustle of the city into our serene sanctuary. Our holistic therapies are designed to relax your mind, ease muscle tension, and rejuvenate your spirit.

  • Swedish & Deep Tissue Massage

    Choose between gentle, relaxing strokes to improve circulation or intense pressure to relieve chronic muscle knots.

  • Aromatherapy Retreat

    A sensory experience using customized blends of warm essential oils to balance your energy and reduce stress.

  • Body Polishing & Wraps

    Full-body exfoliation using organic sea salts, followed by hydrating wraps that leave your skin feeling like silk.

Nail Bar & Essentials

Pamper your hands and feet. Our dedicated nail technicians ensure absolute hygiene while delivering stunning nail art and relaxing manicures.

  • Gel & Acrylic Extensions

    Strong, durable, and beautifully shaped extensions giving you the perfect length and flawless finish.

  • Custom Nail Art

    From minimalist French tips to intricate 3D designs, chrome, and ombre—if you can imagine it, we can create it.

  • Luxury Spa Pedi & Mani

    Includes dead sea salt soaks, cuticle care, deep exfoliation, and an extended massage to relieve tired hands and feet.
