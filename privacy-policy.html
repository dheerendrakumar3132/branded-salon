Privacy Policy

Your trust is our top priority. We strictly protect your personal data.

🔒 Last Updated: July 17, 2026

At Branded Salon ("we", "our", "us"), we are fully committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide to us when you visit our website, book an appointment, or communicate with our team.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best possible salon experience, we collect specific information when you interact with our website or physical salon.

Personal Identification Information

When you book an appointment, subscribe to our newsletter, or fill out a contact form, we may ask for:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Appointment history and service preferences

Non-Personal & Technical Data

Like most premium websites, we automatically collect certain technical data when you browse our site, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, and the pages you visit. This data helps us improve our website's user experience (UX) and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is strictly used to enhance your experience and provide our services efficiently. We use your data to:

  • Confirm & Manage Appointments: Send you booking confirmations, reminders, and updates regarding your scheduled services.
  • Personalize Your Experience: Keep a record of your previous treatments, color formulas, or allergies to serve you better next time.
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries, appointment changes, or feedback effectively.
  • Marketing (With Consent): Send you occasional updates about seasonal offers, new services, or salon news. You can opt out of these emails at any time.

3. Data Protection & Security

Your data security is a serious matter for us. We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information.

Zero Data Selling Policy

We are a premium salon, not a data broker. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to any third parties under any circumstances.

Secure Storage

All sensitive information provided by you is stored in secure, encrypted servers. Access to your personal data is strictly limited to authorized salon management personnel who need the information to perform their specific duties (e.g., front desk managers handling bookings).

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers to help us operate our business and website (such as online booking software, email delivery systems, or payment gateways). These third parties have access to your personal information only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are legally obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

Please note that our website may contain links to external sites (like our Instagram page). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of these external platforms.

5. Your Rights & Control

You have full control over the data you share with us. You have the right to:

  • Access & Review: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update & Correct: Ask us to correct any inaccurate or outdated information (e.g., changing your phone number).
  • Delete: Request the deletion of your personal data from our records (subject to legal or accounting requirements).
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from our marketing communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us directly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please do not hesitate to contact our management team.
