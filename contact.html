Quick Help

Answers to common booking questions

What is your cancellation policy?

We value your time and ours. We request that you cancel or reschedule your appointment at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations may incur a small fee.

Is parking available at the salon?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our clients right in front of the Premium Arcade building.

Absolutely! You can message us on our primary number (+91 98765 43210) through WhatsApp, and our team will confirm your slot based on availability.
