Booking Policies

Please read our guidelines before your visit

⏱️ Arrival Time

Please aim to arrive 10-15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time. This gives you time to check in, enjoy a complimentary beverage, and start your relaxing experience without rushing.

❌ Cancellations

We understand that plans change. We kindly ask for at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations may be subject to a 50% service charge.

🚶‍♀️ Walk-ins

While we operate primarily by appointment to ensure every client gets our full attention, we do welcome walk-ins at our Kanpur branch based on daily availability.
