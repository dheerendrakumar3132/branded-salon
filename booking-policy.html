Booking & Cancellations

Clear guidelines to ensure a smooth and premium experience for everyone.

📅 Last Updated: July 17, 2026

At Branded Salon, we strive to provide the utmost professionalism and premium care to every client. When you book an appointment with us, that time is reserved exclusively for you. We kindly request that you review our booking and cancellation policies below to help us maintain a seamless schedule and respect the time of our expert stylists and other guests.

1. Securing Your Appointment

To ensure fairness and dedication to our scheduled clients, certain services require an advance deposit to secure your booking.

Standard Appointments

For standard services (like haircuts, basic facials, and manicures), no advance deposit is required. However, a valid phone number and email address are mandatory to confirm the slot.

Premium & Bridal Services

For chemical treatments (Keratin, Balayage), large spa packages, and Bridal Makeup, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required at the time of booking. This amount will be credited toward your final bill on the day of your service.

2. Cancellation & Rescheduling

We understand that unexpected events happen and plans change. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, we respectfully ask that you follow our notice period guidelines.

24-Hour Notice (Standard Services)

We require at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule any standard appointment. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice will incur a cancellation fee equal to 50% of the reserved service amount.

48-Hour Notice (Bridal & Group Bookings)

For Bridal packages, large group bookings, or multi-service packages, we require at least 48 hours' notice. If cancelled or rescheduled within 48 hours, the initial 50% deposit will be forfeited.

3. Late Arrival Policy

Timeliness is crucial to providing you and the next client with the best possible service. Please aim to arrive 10 minutes before your scheduled time.

  • Grace Period: We offer a strict 15-minute grace period for late arrivals.
  • After 15 Minutes: If you arrive more than 15 minutes late, we may not be able to perform the full service. Your appointment may be shortened to avoid delaying the next client, but you will still be charged for the full service booked.
  • In severe cases of delay, we may have to completely reschedule your appointment, and a cancellation fee will apply.

4. No-Show Policy

Clients who fail to show up for their scheduled appointment without contacting the salon ("No-Shows") severely impact our daily operations and our stylists' schedules.

Consequences of a No-Show:

  • You will be charged 100% of the reserved service amount.
  • Any advance deposit made will be completely forfeited.
  • Clients with a history of no-shows will be required to pre-pay 100% of their service cost in advance for any future bookings.

5. Refund Guidelines

As a premium service provider, we invest our time and use high-quality products for your treatments.

  • Services: We do not offer cash refunds on any services provided. If you are dissatisfied with your service, please inform us within 48 hours, and we will offer a complimentary correction session.
  • Deposits: All booking deposits for bridal and advanced chemical treatments are strictly non-refundable. They can, however, be transferred to a rescheduled date if the required 48-hour notice is provided.

By booking an appointment with Branded Salon, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to these terms.
