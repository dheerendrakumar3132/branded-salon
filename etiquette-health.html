Etiquette & Health

Maintaining a sanctuary of peace, safety, and luxury for all our guests.

📅 Last Updated: July 17, 2026

Welcome to Branded Salon. We have created a space designed for relaxation, rejuvenation, and artistic excellence. To ensure that every client enjoys a premium experience, and to maintain the highest standards of health and safety, we kindly request all our guests to observe the following etiquette and health guidelines during their visit.

1. Salon Atmosphere & Etiquette

Our salon is a retreat from the busy outside world. Help us keep the vibe peaceful and relaxing for everyone.

Mobile Phones & Devices

To preserve a tranquil environment, we ask that you switch your mobile phones and electronic devices to silent or vibrate mode upon entering. If you must take a call, please keep your voice low or step outside the service area.

Child Safety & Guest Policy

As much as we love children, a salon environment contains sharp tools, hot equipment, and chemicals that can be hazardous. Children under 10 are not permitted in the service areas unless they are receiving a treatment. Additionally, due to limited seating, we kindly request that you do not bring extra companions who are not receiving services.

2. Health & Medical Information

Your well-being is our top priority. Being transparent about your health helps us customize your treatments safely.

Allergies & Medical History

Please inform your stylist or therapist before your service begins if you have any:

  • Skin sensitivities or severe allergies (e.g., nuts, essential oils, hair dye ingredients).
  • Medical conditions such as diabetes, high blood pressure, or heart issues (especially before spa therapies).
  • Pregnancy, as certain massages or chemical treatments may not be recommended.

Patch Testing

For your safety, a patch test is highly recommended (and sometimes mandatory) 24 to 48 hours prior to any major chemical service, such as advanced hair coloring or lash tinting, especially if it is your first time visiting us.

3. Hygiene & Sanitation Commitment

We pride ourselves on adhering to international hygiene protocols to guarantee a safe space for our clients and staff.

Our Promise

  • All metal tools (scissors, clippers, cuticle pushers) are thoroughly washed and sterilized in hospital-grade autoclaves after every single use.
  • Fresh, professionally laundered towels and disposable capes are used for every client.
  • Service stations and spa beds are disinfected immediately after each appointment.

Right to Refuse Service

For the health and safety of our staff and other clients, we reserve the right to refuse service to anyone who appears to have a contagious condition, such as severe head lice, active fungal infections, cold sores, or severe open wounds in the treatment area. We will happily reschedule your appointment once the condition has cleared.

4. Mutual Respect & Professionalism

Branded Salon operates on a foundation of mutual respect. Our team is dedicated to providing you with the best possible service in a courteous manner.

In return, we expect our clients to treat our staff and fellow guests with respect. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy for verbal abuse, inappropriate behavior, or harassment of any kind. Any such behavior will result in the immediate termination of the service without a refund and a permanent ban from the salon.

Thank you for helping us maintain a safe, clean, and beautiful sanctuary.
